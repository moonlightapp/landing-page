How to delete user data

Here are the instructions if you as a moonlight user have decided to delete all your data stored by Moonlight.

Step 1. Go to profile (Third tab)

Step 2. Click the settings icon (top right corner)

Step 3. Then click on Terms and conditions

Step 4. Click Delete all my data

Step 5. A pop up will appear where you click delete and all data will be immidiately deleted from our server
